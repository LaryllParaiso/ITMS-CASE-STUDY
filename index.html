Fibonacci Sequence Calculator



Fibonacci Sequence

The Fibonacci sequence is a series of numbers where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones.

The sequence starts with 0 and 1, and each subsequent number is the sum of the two preceding ones.

For example: 0, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21, ...

Lucas Sequence Calculator



Lucas Sequence

The Lucas sequence is similar to the Fibonacci sequence but starts with 2 and 1 instead of 0 and 1.

Each term is the sum of the two preceding terms. For example: 2, 1, 3, 4, 7, 11, 18, 29, ...

Tribonacci Sequence Calculator



Tribonacci Sequence

The Tribonacci sequence is an extension of the Fibonacci sequence with each term being the sum of the three preceding terms.

It starts with 0, 1, 1, and each subsequent term is the sum of the three preceding terms. For example: 0, 1, 1, 2, 4, 7, 13, 24, ...

Collatz Sequence Calculator



Collatz Sequence

The Collatz sequence is a series of numbers where each term is obtained from the previous term according to a specific rule.

If the current term is even, divide it by 2. If it is odd, multiply it by 3 and add 1.
